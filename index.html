Hi!

I'm a software engineer with a background in creative advertising, motion graphics, and mobile games. I've designed for interactive digital platforms, web/television animation, events, photo shoots, installations, digital/print media campaigns, and more. Throughout this, I worked with many creative developers that used programming as the tool to bring these designs to life.

I became interested in combining my design expertise with a technical skillset, picked up programming basics in my spare time, and completed an intensive fullstack software engineering program. I'm here to address the gap between engineer and design needs during a product's development cycle, and contribute to products that scale effectively and excel in delivering rich user experiences.
