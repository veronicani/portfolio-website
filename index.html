Hi!

I'm a software engineer with a background in creative advertising, motion graphics, and mobile games. Throughout this, I worked with many creative developers that used programming as the tool to bring these designs to life.

I'm here to address the gap between engineer and design needs during a product's development cycle, and contribute to products that scale effectively and excel in delivering rich user experiences.
